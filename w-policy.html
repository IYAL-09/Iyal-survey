Withdrawal Policy

Transparent payout system for all Iyal Surveys users

Minimum Withdrawal

Users must reach the minimum withdrawal threshold before requesting payout. The minimum amount is displayed inside the withdrawal dashboard and may vary depending on rewards and platform updates.

Processing Time

All withdrawal requests are reviewed manually by our admin system. Processing typically takes 24–72 hours depending on request volume.

Approval System

Each withdrawal is subject to verification to ensure fairness, fraud prevention, and system integrity. Approved requests are processed automatically to the user’s account.

Payment Methods

Supported withdrawal methods include bank transfer and other supported digital payment systems as listed in the dashboard.

Fraud Protection

Any attempt to exploit, manipulate, or abuse the reward system will result in account suspension. We monitor IP activity, device behavior, and suspicious actions.

Important Notice

Withdrawal requests may be delayed during system maintenance or high traffic periods. Users are advised to ensure their account details are correct before submitting requests.
