Who We Are

Iyal Surveys is an online rewards platform that connects users with trusted third-party survey and offer providers. Our goal is to give users a simple and transparent way to earn rewards by participating in surveys and completing approved tasks.

What We Do

We partner with reputable advertising and survey networks to display available surveys and offers to our users. When a user completes a survey successfully, we receive a reward from the survey provider, and a portion of that reward is credited to the user’s account.

How It Works

Create an Account

Sign up and access available surveys and offers.

Complete Surveys

Participate in surveys provided by our partners.

Earn Rewards

Earn rewards credited to your account balance.

Important Information

Survey availability, eligibility, and completion status are determined entirely by third-party survey providers. Iyal Surveys does not control survey qualification criteria or disqualifications.

Trust & Transparency

We are committed to operating a transparent platform that respects user privacy and complies with applicable laws and third-party network requirements. Please review our Privacy Policy and Terms & Conditions for more information.
